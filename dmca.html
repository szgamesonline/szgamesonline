2. Our Commitment

SZ Games Online is committed to operating within the bounds of copyright law. We do not intentionally host content that infringes the rights of copyright holders. If content is found to be infringing, we will remove it promptly after receiving a valid DMCA notice. Repeat infringers may be banned from accessing our platform.

3. How to Submit a DMCA Takedown Notice

To file a valid DMCA takedown notice, your request must include all of the following information:

  • Your full legal name, organization (if applicable), and contact information (email, phone, or postal address)
  • A description of the copyrighted work that you claim has been infringed
  • The exact URL(s) on our Site where the infringing content is located
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law
  • A statement that the information in your notice is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner
  • Your physical or electronic signature

Incomplete notices that lack any of the above information may not be processed. Send your complete notice to: muhammadgullsher0786@gmail.com

4. Counter-Notice Procedure

If you believe that content removed from our Site was wrongly taken down or that you have the right to publish it, you may submit a counter-notice. Your counter-notice must include:

  • Your full name and contact information
  • Identification of the content that was removed and the URL where it appeared before removal
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the content was removed in error
  • A statement that you consent to the jurisdiction of the federal court in your district
  • Your physical or electronic signature

Submit counter-notices to: muhammadgullsher0786@gmail.com with subject "DMCA Counter-Notice."

5. Our Response Time

Upon receipt of a valid and complete DMCA notice, we will respond within 2–5 business days. We will make good-faith efforts to remove or disable access to allegedly infringing content promptly. We will notify the party that posted the content of the removal.

6. Repeat Infringers

SZ Games Online has a policy of terminating, in appropriate circumstances, the accounts or access of users who are determined to be repeat infringers. If you have received multiple valid DMCA complaints, we may take action to restrict your access to our platform.

7. False Claims

Please be aware that knowingly making a false claim of copyright infringement may result in liability for damages, including legal costs. If you are unsure whether material infringes your copyright, we recommend seeking legal advice before submitting a DMCA notice.
